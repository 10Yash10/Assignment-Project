## Amplifai Application Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the Amplifai application's functionalities.

### 1. Authentication

The application features a complete authentication system to protect user data and ensure secure access.

#### 1.1. Registration

- To create a new account, navigate to the `/login` page and click the "Register" button.
- You will be prompted to enter your name, business email, and password.
- After successful registration, you will be redirected to the login page to sign in with your new credentials.

#### 1.2. Login

- To log in, navigate to the `/login` page and enter your registered email and password.
- Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the main dashboard.

#### 1.3. Forgot Password

- If you have forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page.
- You will be asked to enter your email address and a new password.
- If the email exists in our records, your password will be updated.

#### 1.4. Protected Routes

- The `dashboard`, `companies`, `users`, and `settings` pages are protected routes.
- You must be logged in to access these pages. If you are not logged in, you will be automatically redirected to the `/login` page.

### 2. Dashboard

The dashboard provides a high-level overview of your business data.

#### 2.1. Year Selector and Line Chart

- The dashboard features a year selector that allows you to view data for different financial years.
- When you select a year, the line chart will update to display the revenue and profit trends for that year.

#### 2.2. Insights Section

- The "Insights" section provides key highlights and summaries of your business performance.
- Company names within the insights are highlighted for easy identification.

### 3. Companies Page

The companies page allows you to manage and view a list of companies.

#### 3.1. Viewing Companies

- This page displays a paginated list of companies with key information such as CEO, revenue, profit, and more.

#### 3.2. Adding New Companies

- You can add a new company to the list by clicking the "Add Company" button.
- A modal will appear where you can enter the new company's details.

#### 3.3. Searching and Filtering

- You can search for specific companies using the search bar.
- You can also filter the list by different criteria (e.g., company name, revenue) using the filter button.

### 4. Loading State

- When the application is fetching data or transitioning between pages, a rotating loader will be displayed to indicate that the application is busy.

### 5. Logout

- To log out, click the profile icon at the bottom of the side navigation bar.
- A dropdown menu will appear with a "Logout" button.
- Clicking "Logout" will clear your session and redirect you to the login page.
